GLOBAL events & demonstrations

The global action of solidarity with Russian political prisoners traditionally takes place in mid-January and is timed to coincide with the date of Alexei Navalny's arrest. This year's action will take place on January 21, with some of its events coordinated by the Anti-Corruption Foundation.
On February 24 and 25, the date of the beginning of Russia's full-scale war against Ukraine, Russians will get together in many cities around the world to once again demonstrate solidarity with the people of Ukraine and declare their rejection of the Russian state's military aggression and terrorist actions.

The list of events is above.
The global action is dedicated to protesting against illegal methods of pre-election campaigning, the voting process itself and against the usurpation of power by Vladimir Putin.
A list of events will soon be on the website.
