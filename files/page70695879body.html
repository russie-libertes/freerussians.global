#NoPutin
The 12th of June, a day usurped by Putin's regime and designated as "Russia Day," was declared by us as Free Russia Day in the first year of full-scale war.
We are the real Russia, not the aggressor state with a fake president who tries to speak on our behalf. We value human rights, freedom of thought and speech, and the rejection of aggression and repression.
On June 12, Russians around the world traditionally hold events. Most Free Russia Day 2025 events aim to help political prisoners, the freest people living in conditions of total bondage. These events include evenings of letters in support of the "You Are Not Alone" marathon organized by several independent media outlets; pickets, rallies, and flash mobs as part of the Free Them All project, which was initiated by the French association Russie Libertés; and other activities.
