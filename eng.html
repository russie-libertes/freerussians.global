Free Russians Global is the union of anti-war and democratic communities of Russians worldwide. The cooperation of Russian activists takes place at all levels: from simple tips or exchange of ideas to joint actions of various scales. Organizers and activist groups located in different parts of the world interact with each other on a daily basis, forming a horizontal decentralized association.
All activities of Free Russians Global are carried out on a volunteer basis. This informal association plays the role of a media center, an information center and a center for coordinating collaborative events.
Actions and events are prepared, coordinated and implemented on the initiative, by the forces and at the expense of Russian activists around the world and are not projects of any of the existing organizations or structures.
