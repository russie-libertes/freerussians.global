On December 1, anti-war and democratic communities of Russians around the world, together with the human rights project "Political prisoners support. Memorial" launched a fundraising campaign called "New Year's Gift for Political Prisoners." While we turn on the lights and set the tables for the holiday season, hundreds of political prisoners in Russia will spend New Year's Eve in cold prison cells, away from their families and loved ones and the comforts of home. We can provide these prisoners with the most important things: support, safety, and human warmth. Every €5–10 donation provides real help to those behind bars. Donate whatever you can right now!
What will the donations be used for?
- Replenishing prisoners' personal accounts for purchases in prison kiosks
- Parcels with warm clothes and essential items
- Calls from prison to family and friends
- Long visits with family
- Legal support and defense
Let's remind all in jail that they are not alone and make prison cells a little warmer. Walls are no barrier to human feelings, care, and solidarity.
WHO ARE WE?
The global action is prepared, coordinated and implemented by the initiative of united Russian activists around the world and is not a project of any existing organization or structure. We are raising funds in conjunction with the human rights project "Political prisoners support. Memorial."
