On February 22-24, 2025, independent anti-war and democratic Russian communities will hold a global action, “Three Years of Terror: Save Ukraine to Save the World."
Three years ago, on February 24, 2022, Putin’s government launched a full-scale invasion of Ukraine. On this date, Russians around the world will take to the streets to express solidarity with the people of Ukraine and mourn those killed in this criminal war.
Participants in this global action will call on Western leaders to maintain unwavering support for Ukraine and provide the country with the necessary security guarantees.
As part of this global campaign, activists are raising funds to provide portable power stations for hospitals and schools in Ukraine through the initiative “Energy for Life.”
Additionally, participants will support the “People First” campaign, an international effort advocating for the release of civilians forcibly detained as a result of Russia’s invasion of Ukraine.

Energy for life

LET'S RAISING FUNDS FOR PORTABLE POWER PLANTS FOR UKRAINE

I will help Ukraine
Why it matters
Ongoing attacks on Ukraine's energy infrastructure mean millions suffer regular power outages. Hospitals are in a critical situation: without electricity, medical equipment cannot operate and patients' lives are at risk. Schools cannot provide a normal education for children. Temperatures can drop to -17°C in winter.

How your help can make a difference
Your donations will help purchase and deliver portable generators to hospitals and schools in Ukraine, saving lives and allowing children to continue their studies even in war.
